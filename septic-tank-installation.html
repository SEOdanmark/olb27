Understanding the different types of septic systemsSeptic systems are an essential component of many homes, particularly in rural areas where connection to a municipal sewer system is not possible.

There are several different types of septic systems, each with its own unique characteristics and requirements. Understanding the differences between these systems is important for homeowners to ensure proper maintenance and functionality. One common type of septic system is the conventional gravity system.

Once inside the tank, solid waste settles to the bottom while liquids flow out through an outlet pipe into a drain field for further treatment. Another type of septic system is the pressure distribution system. This system uses a pump to evenly distribute effluent throughout the drain field, allowing for more efficient treatment of wastewater.
